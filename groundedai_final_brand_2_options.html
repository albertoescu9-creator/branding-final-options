Two refined options based on consolidated team feedback. Both address: high contrast, warm tones (not yellow, not blue), iPad visibility in underground conditions, bold but technical aesthetic. Choose one, close it this week.

Option A · Warm Amber — Refined

#E3A82A — The amber direction refined per feedback: richer saturation (less "pure yellow"), warmer undertone, stronger on white backgrounds. This is the direction the team responded most positively to on the homepage. Deepened from #E3B23C → #E3A82A for more warmth and better contrast ratios.
